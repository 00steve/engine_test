§ AddForce()

void PhysicalSingleBody::AddForce ( const double3  force)
virtual

adds a force at the COG of an object.

The force is added at the COG(center of gravity) of the object. This force causes acceleration, but not rotational change because it is acting on the center of gravity. If you want to simulate push or some type of force acting on an object that could cause velocity or rotational change, use the addForceAtPoint() function
